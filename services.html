common questions

FAQs

Do I need to buy anything before my session?

Nope! I'll assess your closet during the consultation and recommend products only if needed. Most closets can be dramatically improved with just what you already have.

What do I need to do to prepare?

Nothing special — I actually prefer to see your closet as-is so I can understand how it's being used. Just make sure I can access the space.

Will you make me get rid of things?

Never. The decisions are always yours. I'm here to help you think through what's working and what isn't — but you're always in control of what stays and what goes.

Do you serve areas outside Charlotte?

Yes! I serve Charlotte and surrounding areas. A small travel fee may apply for locations more than 20 miles from Charlotte. Reach out and we'll figure it out.
