the person behind the process

hi, i'm cerrae

intentional living no-judgment zone functional beauty charlotte, nc

I started Curated by Cerrae because I truly believe that an organized space changes how you feel every single day. Clutter creates stress. Order creates calm.

Based in Charlotte, NC, I work with busy families, professionals, and anyone ready to finally love their home again. I know what it feels like to look around a room and feel overwhelmed — and I know the relief of walking into a space that finally works for you.

My approach is personal. I don't come in with a rigid system and impose it on your life. I listen first. I learn how you actually move through your home, what your habits are, and what you've tried before. Then we build something that fits you — not a magazine.

No judgment. Ever. I've seen it all, and my job isn't to make you feel bad about the mess — it's to help you move past it.
